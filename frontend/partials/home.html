Flood warnings checker

Welcome to Flood warnings checker. You can check the latest River or Sea flood warnings and also historical warnings raised by the Environment Agency. We display flood areas on maps and display data available for each warning. You can also search for warnings by keyword! (I can't believe I could be bothered to do that! made easier by some node package or other :)

We only record alerts with a minimum severity level of 'Flood Warning' here which is the econd most severe warning. 'Severe Flood Warnings' will also be displayed. You can visit the Enviroment Agency's website to see all Flood warnings issued with lower severity levels.

There are currently no flood warnings in place. Visit the warnings page to view all previously recorded Flood warnings.
